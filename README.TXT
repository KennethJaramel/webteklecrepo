Instruction on how to set up the site

* Click the index.html in our folder
* Once you clicked it, you will see our website
* The Navigation bar contains the HOME, PRELIM, MIDTERM, FINALS, GLOSSARY, and OUR TEAM
* Our Home page contains the overview of the Prelims and Midterm
* Click the Prelim and Midterm in our navigation bar and it will be redirected to their respective pages (prelim.html, midterm.html)
* Once you click those, you will see all the topics discussed in prelim and midterm
* The content of the "Our Team" in the navigation bar is pictures of our members
